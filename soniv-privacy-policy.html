Privacy Policy for TuneMesh

Effective Date: August 17, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TuneMesh, an offline music player designed to provide you with a seamless and secure music experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

TuneMesh is designed to play music that is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates solely with the music files you have on your device and does not access any external data or services.

3. Data Usage

As TuneMesh does not collect any personal information, there is no data usage beyond the local operation of the app. Your music files remain on your device and are not shared with any third parties.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your local data and music files are kept secure while using TuneMesh. However, as we do not store or transmit data, our security measures are focused on protecting the integrity of the app's functionality.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.
